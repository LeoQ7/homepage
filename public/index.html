Bio

I am a first-year Computer Science Master student in System and Software Security Lab (S3L ) at ShanghaiTech University , advised by Prof. Fu Song . Also, I intern at Tencent’s Keen Security Lab .

My research interest spans broadly in computer security, including program analysis and AI security.

I’ve participated in a lot of CTF competitions. I am currently a member of Nu1L Team . I mainly solve Crypto and Misc challenges in CTFs, I also developed a great interest in reverse engineering.

I obtained my B.E. degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University in July 2020.
